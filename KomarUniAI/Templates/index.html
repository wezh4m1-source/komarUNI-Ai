About Komar Uni AI

Smart University Assistant

Komar Uni AI is an intelligent chatbot built specifically for students and faculty at KUST (Komar University of Science and Technology). Our AI combines advanced language models with a comprehensive knowledge base to provide accurate, research-backed answers to your questions.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower KUST students and faculty with accessible AI-powered educational support that promotes learning, research, and innovation.

🌟 Key Features

  • Educational Support: Help with programming, AI, and academic topics
  • Research-Backed: Information is verified and sourced
  • 24/7 Available: Always ready to help when you need it
  • Learning Resources: Links to official documentation and courses
  • KUST Integration: Specialized knowledge about our university
